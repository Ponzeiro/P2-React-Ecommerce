# **App Name**: BeauteSphere

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Browsing: Users can view a catalog of cosmetic products, categorized for easy navigation.
- Detailed Product View: Click on a product to see detailed information, including descriptions, ingredients, and usage instructions.
- Shopping Cart Management: Users can add, remove, and adjust quantities of products in their shopping cart before checkout.
- Secure User Authentication: Allow users to register accounts, log in, and manage their basic profile information, interacting with the Spring Boot backend.
- Checkout Workflow: A streamlined process for users to confirm their order and proceed to a placeholder payment stage.
- AI-Powered Skincare Recommendation Tool: Based on user-provided input such as skin type and concerns, an AI tool suggests a personalized skincare routine and suitable products from the catalog.
- Backend API Integration: Seamless integration with the Spring Boot backend for all product, user, and order data retrieval and submission.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme, evoking a clean and natural feel, suitable for showcasing cosmetic products.
- Primary Color: Muted Rose (#C2989D). A sophisticated, gentle hue for interactive elements and highlights, suggesting elegance and warmth.
- Background Color: Pale Blush (#F9F0F1). A very light, desaturated variant of the primary color, providing a soft, unobtrusive backdrop that enhances product visibility.
- Accent Color: Vibrant Berry (#BF3380). A bolder, analogous color used for critical calls-to-action and important alerts, providing a striking contrast while maintaining harmony.
- Headline Font: 'Playfair' (modern serif). Selected for its elegant, high-contrast lines that evoke a sense of luxury and high-end fashion.
- Body Font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif). Chosen for its readability and modern feel, complementing the headline font without clashing, ideal for product descriptions and general text.
- Use minimalist, clean line-art icons that align with the sophisticated aesthetic of cosmetic branding.
- Embrace a spacious and clean layout with ample white space to highlight product imagery and ensure ease of navigation.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects and transition animations on product cards and buttons for an interactive and polished user experience.
